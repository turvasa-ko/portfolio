My Story

Ever since I was a child, I've been fascinated by technology. My first real exposure came in middle school, when we practiced to use computers and built simple circuits. This inspired me to choose optional subjects in high school like Computers Handcraft and Electricity Handcraft, where I first got to try coding. We first started with trivial Arduino projects and Scratch games, and even though they were basic, I found them incredibly fun and rewarding.

In upper secondary school I was determined to take my first "real" coding class, when we had an optional course following the Elementary Programming course from the Univeristy of Oulu. At the time, it felt extremely challenging, but I stuck with it. Nowadays, coding is fun, but also quite addictive, and it continues to drive my curiosity and passion for building new projects.
